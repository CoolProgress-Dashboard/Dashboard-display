CoolProgress · London walk-through cheat-sheet

20-min CCC briefing · Part 2 of 3 (live walk-through) · ~2 min per pillar. Keep on phone or second screen. The dashboard does the visual lifting — your job is the connective tissue between pillars.

~2 min

Pillar 1 · Emissions

/dashboard/emissions

Action: Land on a country relevant to the room (India / Nigeria / Brazil / Indonesia). Click in to show direct vs indirect split.

Anchor stat: Cooling is ~7% of global GHG today. 70% is INDIRECT (electricity), 30% direct (refrigerant leaks).

Key message: "These are two different problems with two different policy responses — efficiency standards on the indirect side, Kigali compliance on the direct side. Most ministries still treat them as one. The dashboard shows why they shouldn't."

~2 min

Pillar 2 · Product Efficiency (MEPS)

/dashboard/meps

Action: Open the MEPS map. Pick an African country for the data-gap honest. Scroll to the inverter penetration chart.

Anchor stat: Best AC = 3× more efficient than the worst still legally sold. Global MEPS harmonisation = ~1,300 TWh/year saved. India 31→82% inverter penetration in 5 years; USA still 9%.

Key message: "Without binding standards, manufacturers send the worst products to the markets growing fastest. This pillar surfaces CLASP-owned data so a minister can see exactly where their market sits."

~2 min

Pillar 3 · Refrigerant Transition / Kigali

/dashboard/kigali

Action: Open the Kigali ratification map. Show the refrigerant bank chart.

Anchor stat: 172 countries have ratified. Full compliance prevents ~0.5°C of warming this century. Global refrigerant bank still growing: 5–6 Mt circulating today.

Key message: "Ratification is the easy part. The dashboard tracks whether countries are actually following through — Article 5 Group 1, Group 2, and Non-A5 timelines side by side. Sourced from the Ozone Secretariat and COPA/GIZ."

~2 min

Pillar 4 · Access & Vulnerability

/dashboard/access

Action: Open the access map. Show the regional bar chart.

Anchor stat: 1.2 billion face dangerous heat without adequate cooling. 420,000 deaths/year from broken cold chains. 40% post-harvest food loss in Sub-Saharan Africa. SSA 450M, South Asia 380M, SE Asia 170M.

Key message: "This pillar reframes cooling as health and food security, not luxury. Built on SEforALL Chilling Prospects data, surfaced country-by-country so a minister can see exactly where their access gap sits."

~2 min

Pillar 5 · National Plans & Commitments

/dashboard/policy

Action: Open the policy page. Show signatory map and NDC mentions. Be honest about the live update in progress.

Anchor stat: 74 countries + 235 cities signed the Global Cooling Pledge at COP28 — the largest political commitment ever on cooling.

Key message + bridge: "NDC cooling mentions and National Cooling Action Plans are being updated via CLASP and Climate Policy Radar — refreshes in progress. This is where your contribution helps most directly. Honest gap, easy ask — which transitions us into Part 3."

Part 3 · The three questions for the room

  1. Which pillar earns its keep for YOUR mandate? — tells us where to invest in depth next.
  2. What data layer is missing? — honest gap-naming. NDC mentions, NCAPs, sub-national, sectoral?
  3. Who wants to co-shape phase 2? — co-branding, co-funding, data partnership, ministry partnership.
