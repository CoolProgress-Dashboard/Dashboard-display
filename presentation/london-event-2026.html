The collaboration

It's not anyone's dashboard. It's everyone's.

CoolProgress is a coalition product, not a vendor product. Each partner brings the data they own. HEAT GmbH orchestrates — meaning we hold the platform, harmonise the streams, and make sure the experience stays usable for a non-technical decision-maker.

ORCHESTRATOR

HEAT GmbH — platform, data integration, design, partner coordination, narrative architecture.

FUNDER

Clean Cooling Collaborative (ClimateWorks) — strategic framing and funding for phases 1 and 2.

EFFICIENCY

CLASP — MEPS, energy labels, NDC Appliance Toolkit, inverter market intelligence.

REFRIGERANTS

GIZ Proklima · Ozone Secretariat — Kigali ratification, A5 timelines, refrigerant-bank data.

ACCESS

SEforALL — Chilling Prospects access and vulnerability data, country-level cooling-gap mapping.

SCENARIOS

IEA · LBNL — cooling demand projections, efficiency scenarios, end-use disaggregation.

POLICY

Climate Policy Radar — National Cooling Action Plans, NDC excerpts, policy-text tracking.

+ YOU

Phase 2 partners — data contributors, ministry partners, additional funders. That's what today's meeting is about.
